Titanic Dataset EDA Report
1. Overview
   - Dataset contains 891 rows and 12 columns
   - Missing values are handled for 'Age' (filled with median) and 'Embarked' ( Filled with mode).

2. Key Insights.
   - Surival rates are highest for first - class passengers (62%) and lower for third-class customers (24%)
   - Majority of passengers are aged between 20-40 years
   - A positive correlation exists between fare and surival
